Turfpark

Designer 2018-19

Turfpark is a sports facilities brand improving the experience of budding sports enthusiast in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities.

  • Know more

Role

I was primarily responsible for conceptualizing & desiging the graphics used for social media post.

Problem

Turfpark has sports facilities all over India, hence to encourage more people (via social media) to play at sports facilities was difficult as people prefered grounds instead of turf.

Solution

Minimalist graphics design was the approach I took to make simple, creative & impactful social media post that will be memorable & well suited for the brand.

Process

1
Identifying the key requirements
Listing all the important elements which can be used.
2
Generating ideas
Thinking back & forth to visualized how can those elements can be used.
3
Design
Building the design and refining the work.

Process

Target Audience

Our audience aged from 9years to 45years who has passion for sports and fitness.

Personality

Energetic, friendly, supportive, togetherness, youth, prosperity, comfort.

Campaigns

We noticed our target audience was youth and energetic and hence to adapt instagram profile with engaging and energetic content, we came up with few marketing campaigns.

  • Audience are following their favourite team/sport/ sports player. Hence creating content relatable to that can make them feel connected to us. Celebrating sports player's birthday or achievements, their life struggles are few points which can be done.
  • Festival wishes - We tried to mix sports elements with the festival keeping the whole festival message constant and impactful for the sports enthusiast.
  • News & trends were a key point which they are interested on daily basics.
  • Sports Puns - To create a brand personality friendly we incorporated humorous content.

Learnings

Adapting minimalism in design was a win- win for everybody. Minimal post were liked more than other post. Hence keeping things simple and impactful can increased engagement on social media and it will be memorable and recognizable which is a plus point for branding.

I grew as a designer and better understood how refining thoughts and removing noise can be useful to create impactful design. Delivering a wide message with less words and elements was a complex thing.

But, perhaps unparalleled, were the learnings from the brand side: the failures, the successes, and the in-betweens.
