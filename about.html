A little about me.

A passionate Designer focused on enabling innovation through digital. I have a strong understanding of how Graphic Design integrates with an overall brand and its marketing efforts. Also, a deep passion for user-centered design, dedicated to making the best use of design expertise to craft meaningful experiences and products for humans.

Motivated by Design Thinking, and equally fascinated by the power of human connection and it's ability to energies, transform and catalyze change, especially within fields of expertise.

As being obsessed with the intersection of design and blockchain, I’m embarking on a journey to create some principles for designing blockchain applications.

I’m going to use design thinking methods and an iterative research/synthesize/learn process to throw some data behind the principles and see if they are effective.

Outside of technology I like reading, writing and love learning and knowing in-depth about different sports and musical instruments.
